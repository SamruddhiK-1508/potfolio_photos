The Artist Behind the Magic

A story born from passion


I'm Suman Kokade — a certified luxury makeup artist with over 10 years of experience transforming brides across Solapur, Pune, Mumbai, and beyond. My journey began with a simple childhood wonder: how a brush and a little colour could make someone feel completely, breathtakingly themselves.

Trained at the prestigious VLCC and internationally certified in airbrush and HD techniques, I've had the privilege of being part of over 350 bridal stories — each one unique, each one sacred. I believe every bride carries her own kind of beauty; my art is simply to illuminate it.

I use only premium, skin-safe products from MAC, Charlotte Tilbury, and NARS. Because on your most important day, your skin deserves nothing but the finest.
